New Jurassic World Film Officially Confirmed

Two years after the release of Jurassic World Dominion, the Jurassic franchise is back. The Jurassic World Twitter page released a teaser image of the Jurassic logo and a date of July 2, 2025, as an official confirmation. David Koepp will return as screenwriter, with Steve Brusatte as the Paleontologist consultant for this film.
